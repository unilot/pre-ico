What is Unilot

Who we are

Unilot is a decentralized gambling platform which allows players to play and host online games like poker tournaments, lottery and many other games. Unilot will be available through mobile apps on OSx and Android platforms and transact using Unilots as it’s main currency.

Unilot Benefits

Benefits

Unilot will not access or hold any Player Funds

Consensus of game state is reached between players with encrypted P2P messaging

Play games globally or make money by hosting your own game tournaments

Feature Single player RNG & Multiplayer games

Decintrilized and isolated to prevent God Mode

Unilot platform can be Whitelabeled

Mobile Client

Mobile App

Unilot Mobile Client will be available on iOS and Android platforms. Unilot mobile will support multiple regions, languages offered English, Russian and German
