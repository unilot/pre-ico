Bounty UNILOT

Official unilot.io bounty campaign terms:

 

UTN ICO starts on January 10, 2017 and we are happy to announce the official unilot.io bounty program details.

The total bounty program pool will amount to 2% of all tokens sold during the pre-ICO and ICO stages.

 

If the number of bounty participants will be less than  250  active users - total bounty pool will be reduced to 1%.  Active user - the person who made at least one activity in bounty program.

The Unilot.io total bounty pool will be divided as follows:

 

  1. Bitcointalk — 20%
  2. Translation and community management — 10%
  3. Facebook — 10%
  4. Twitter —10%
  5. Youtube — 6%
  6. Reddit  — 3%
  7. Telegram —10%
  8. Mass media and Blogs—17%
  9. LinkedIn — 4%
  10. Exclusive support — 5%
  11. Reserve - 5%

Bounty payment

Payment is made only in Unilot.io tokens – UTN

Bounty program’s results, including the calculation of tokens earned, will be announced within one week after the token launch end date and time.

Each member's amount of bounty UTN tokens will be calculated using the following formula below.

 

(Total amount of bounty campaign UTN tokens /  Total amount of bounty campaign participants' stakes)  *  Member's stakes

For example, the distribution of tokens in the signature campaign:

(Total amount of signature campaign UTN tokens /  Total amount of bounty signature campaign participants' stakes)  *  Member's stakes
