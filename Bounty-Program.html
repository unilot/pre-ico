Bounty UNILOT

Official unilot.io bounty campaign terms:

UNILOT ICO starts on January 10, 2017 and we are happy to announce the official unilot.io bounty program details.

The total bounty program pool will amount to 3% of all tokens sold during the pre-ICO and ICO stages.

If the number of bounty participants will be less than 250 active users - total bounty pool will be reduced to 1%. Active user - the person who made at least one activity in bounty program.

The Unilot.io total bounty pool will be divided as follows:

  1. Bitcointalk — 20%
  2. Translation and community management — 10%
  3. Facebook — 10%
  4. Twitter — 10%
  5. Youtube — 6%
  6. Reddit — 3%
  7. Telegram — 10%
  8. Mass media and Blogs — 17%
  9. LinkedIn — 4%
  10. Exclusive support — 5%
  11. Reserve — 5%

Bounty payment

Payment is made only in Unilot.io tokens – UNILOT

Bounty program’s results, including the calculation of tokens earned, will be announced within one week after the token launch end date and time.

Each member's amount of bounty UNILOT tokens will be calculated using the following formula below.

(Total amount of bounty campaign UNILOT tokens / Total amount of bounty campaign participants' stakes) * Member's stakes

For example, the distribution of tokens in the signature campaign:

(Total amount of signature campaign UNILOT tokens / Total amount of bounty signature campaign participants' stakes) * Member's stakes

Bitcointalk

20% of Total Bounty Pool will be allocated for signature and avatar campaign participants.

Bounty:

  1. Member: 1 Stake / Week
  2. Full Member: 1.5 Stakes / Week
  3. Sr. Member: 1.75 Stakes / Week
  4. Hero Member: 2 Stakes / Week
  5. Legendary: 2.5 Stakes / Week

Bonuses:

+0.25 stakes per week, if you use Unilot.io avatar + Unilot.io personal text.

Rules:

  • Keep the signature (as well as the avatar and the personal text if you picked them as well) till the end of campaign. We will check your messages on a regular basis to make sure you are using our signature (and avatar and personal text, if applicable) at all times.
  • Only posts in the alternate cryptocurrencies sections of Bitcointalk are required and counted.
  • Make at least 10 quality posts in a week to be eligible for a reward. Your first week is 7 days starting from the date of approval (see the spreadsheet); your second week is the next 7 days, and so on.
  • Using multiple signatures at the same time is prohibited, only Unilot.io signature.
  • You are not allowed to use any other project’s avatar or personal text.
  • You must update the signature, the personal text, and the avatar immediately upon our request.
  • You must make sure that the signature you are wearing corresponds to your current rank at all times. When your account gets promoted to a higher rank during your participation, you must upgrade the signature.
  • Posts must be constructive and on-topic. Spam, low-quality posts, copypaste, and off-topic posts are strictly prohibited. Users found doing so will be immediately and permanently disqualified, reported to Bitcointalk moderators, and banned from any other bounty campaign.
  • Eligible posts must be at least 50 characters long.
  • We recommend keeping your signature till the final spreadsheet gets updated with your final post count (allow at least one week to count posts). Removing signature before post count may lead to your disqualification.

Unilot.io reserves the right to change the terms or apply new ones at any time at its sole discretion.

Facebook

10% social media bounty campaign pool will be used for Facebook.

Earn tokens as a reward for helping us spread the word about Unilot.io on Facebook by means of posts, likes, and reposts.

Bounty

Standard (For users who have at least 300 and not more 1 000 followers)

  • 1 post about Unilot.io with a link to the official website – 1 Stake.
  • 1 like and 1 repost of 1 post from the official page of Unilot.io on Facebook – 1 Stake.

Medium (For users who have at least 1 001 and not more than 2 000 followers)

  • 1 post about Unilot.io with a link to the official website – 3 Stakes.
  • 1 like and 1 repost of 1 post from the official page of Unilot.io on Facebook – 3 Stakes.

Gold (For users who have at least 2 001 and not more than 5 000 followers)

  • 1 post about Unilot.io with a link to the official website – 7 Stakes.
  • 1 like and 1 repost of 1 post from the official page of Unilot.io on Facebook – 7 Stakes.

Platinum (For users who have at least 4 001 and not more than 10 000 followers)

  • 1 post about Unilot.io with a link to the official website – 21 Stake.
  • 1 like and 1 repost of 1 post from the official page of Unilot.io on Facebook – 21 Stakes.

If you have more than 10 001 subscribers, your remuneration for the activity will be considered on an individual basis.

Extra bonus for comments and reposts

Level 1

  • If you have at least 5 and not more than 30 comments on your post - 1 Stake
  • If you have at least 5 and not more than 30 reposts on your post - 1 Stake

Level 2

  • If you have at least 31 and not more than 100 comments on your post - 3 Stakes
  • If you have at least 31 and not more than 100 reposts on your post - 3 Stakes

Level 3

  • If you have at least 101 and not more than 300 comments on your post - 9 Stakes
  • If you have at least 101 and not more than 300 reposts on your post - 9 Stakes

Rules

  • Only one Facebook account per person is allowed.
  • Your Facebook account must have at least 300 real followers.
  • You have to like and repost at least 5 posts from our Facebook account: https://www.facebook.com/unilot.io/. Only 1 like + 1 repost per day will count.
  • You have to make 3 Facebook posts about Unilot.io in a week, include hashtag #Unilot.io. Only 1 post per day will count.
  • You cannot remove your posts, likes or reposts you made within the framework of this Facebook bounty campaign until its end plus one week. This will make sure you quickly pass all checks.

How to join

Sign up on our official website of the bounty program: https://unilot.io/en-us/cp/sign-up.html and find out the Facebook bounty campaign and complete form: https://drive.google.com/open?id=1yZBhL_NK4t9-2gkP6rfovj40iDTN1LopBBFuF0SgNi0
